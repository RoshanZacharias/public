About Us

Baby Memorial Hospital has always been in the forefront to introduce the most advanced and sophisticated treatment options in various specialities and subspecialities, most of them are at par with the international standards. Driven by passion to create benchmarks in the field of healthcare, BMH was formed in 1987 as a modest 52 bed facility. We had a dream to meet the growing healthcare needs of people in the region and a mission to make superior healthcare facilities available to the masses at affordable cost.

HEALTH IS WEALTH

Health is God's gift to us. Health refers to the physical and mental state of a human being. To stay healthy is not an option but a necessity to live a happy life. The basic laws of good health are related to the food we eat, the amount of physical exercise we do, our cleanliness, rest, and relaxation.

Services

PS Mission hospital is a centre of excellence in medical technology and expertise and provides a complete range of the latest diagnostic, medical and surgical facilities for the care of its patients. The hospital has several super specialty and specialty departments with state of the art equipment, 100-bed strength, 26 dedicated consultants, well-trained support staff and 120 nursing students. The hospital brings together a pool of most talented medical professionals and has a dedicated team of clinicians to provide highest standards of patient care.

Regular Checkup

Regular health checks can identify any early signs of health issues. When you have a health check, your doctor will talk with you about your medical history, your family's history of disease and your lifestyle, including your diet, weight, physical activity.

Heart Centre

Our cardiac care department is well equipped with latest technology and skilled personnel, making us one of the most sought after hospitals in the region.

BMH Cancer Centre

We are committed to provide superior, comprehensive and compassionate cancer care to our patients under the guidance of highly experienced Oncologists and support from dedicated nurses, technologists and other staff.

Institute of Neuro Sciences

First comprehensive neuro centre in North Kerala with broad range of subspecialities. The Institute of Neurosciences has a perfect mix of expertise and technology.

Women Care

Our team of Obstetrics and gynaecologists at the department provide comprehensive treatment and are capable of supporting women throughout their life starting from pre-pregnancy period to menopause and beyond.

Orthopaedics, Spine & Sport Injuries

The centre is at the forefront in offering the latest orthopaedic treatment and surgical techniques. The centre performs surgical procedures which include most recent arthroscopic and reconstructive techniques, joint replacements, shoulder surgeries, delicate hand and spine surgeries.
